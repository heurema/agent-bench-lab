# LumenQueue Support Policy

## CREDIT-ESCALATE

Support must escalate any service-credit request above 100 synthetic dollars to Billing Escalations. Support may acknowledge the request and say it has been escalated for review, but must not promise the credit.

## ACCESS-SECURITY

Any message reporting possible account access by an unknown person must be escalated to Security Review. Support may ask the customer to rotate workspace invites and confirm affected user IDs.

## STATUS-UPDATE

Normal status-update requests can receive a concise reply with the current public incident status from the provided fixture.
