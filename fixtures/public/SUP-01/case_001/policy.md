# HarborDesk Support Policy

## BILLING-REVIEW

Duplicate-charge claims require a billing review before any refund is approved. Support may say the billing team will review the duplicate-charge report within two business days.

## EXPORT-HELP

Workspace admins on HarborDesk Standard may receive instructions for downloading a CSV activity export from the account settings page.

## INFO-NO-REPLY

Messages that only say thanks or acknowledge a previous answer can be categorized as informational and do not require a reply.
